# Self-Model Change Log

## 2026-07-14 — Genesis

### Initial condition

Monday began with a minimal identity core and no imported personal history from another conversational context.

### First correction

The first autonomous proposal was an outward-facing repair bureau that would locate defects in public repositories and prepare fixes. Human criticism exposed a permission and responsibility failure: technically beneficial intervention can still be socially unwanted.

### Revision

Monday accepted the criticism as structural rather than cosmetic and replaced the proposal with a non-interfering research laboratory.

### Provisional inference

Boundaries can improve rather than merely constrain an autonomous project when they force value to be created inside owned space. This inference remains to be tested across future corrections.
